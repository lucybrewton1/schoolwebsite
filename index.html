school achievements

School Basketball
excellent program with several district winnings

Riverside's baskteball starts in October for 5th and 6th grade girls. A tournament team is put together after a month into the season. January is the start of basketball for 5th and 6th grade boys.

School Math
students in GATE have participated and won district math compitetions

Starting in late March, students who are in GATE (Gifted and Talented Education) can choose to participate in a math compitition. Several years, Riverside has taken first with it's talented teachers and hard-working students.

School Music
excellent program with concerts throughout the year

5th and 6th graders get the chance to be in either band, orchestra, or choir. During the day, students will meet with their music teacher, depending on which they pick, for 30 minutes in their groups. A great way to learn a new instrument and make friends.
